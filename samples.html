Stochastik Samples

Stochastik features over a thousand included drum, cymbal and percussion sounds.

Featuring:

  • TR-808. About a hundred samples, including 25 Bass drum samples and 25 snare drum samples so you can have exactly the 808 sound you are looking for.
  • TR-909. Over two hundred samples, including 50 bass drum samples and 60 snare drum samples, varying the attack, decay and tone. One of the most thorough selections of 909 samples.
  • TR-606. Sampled both regular and accent variations of each sound.
  • Acoustic - Basement. Over 225 sounds of your typical basement rock kit.
  • Acoustic - Easy Rider. Over 115 pristine samples at varying levels of intensity. Excellent for pop, rock or jazz.
  • Acoustic - Jazz. Over 200 samples of a jazz kit with multiple hits for each drum and cymbal, with snares on and off.
  • Acoustic - Rough Rider. Over 60 samples of this punchy and in-your-face kit that's great for metal, hard rock and punk.
  • Jungle - The classic Amen break cut up and ready to kick it.
  • 8bit - over a hundred classic c64, glitch and video game-style hits, bleeps and bloops for all your techno, electro and glitch needs.
  • Dubstep - Over 50 Deep basses, face-smacking snares and cutting cymbals will make your drops land like a 50 megaton warhead of awesome.

All of the samples in Stochastik have been licensed or have been released in the public domain and are offered royalty-free. You are free to use the samples and included patterns in your original musical compositions, but may not redistribute as single hits or sample packs.
