What makes Stochastik different?

Stochastik is a new kind of drum machine where you set a probability for each step.  Now, you can express musical concepts like: "have the snare on the upbeat of 4 about 30% of the time."  You can generate millions of variations from a single pattern.  You can also increase your expressiveness by creating patterns up to 8 bars in length.

Finally, a drum machine that makes it easy to have tons of variation.
No more boring loops.
