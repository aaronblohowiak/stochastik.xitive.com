What makes Stochastik different?

You can generate millions of variations from a single pattern. We start with a fantastic drum machine and add the power of a Stochastik Process to generate dynamic beats. In the time it takes to set up a single measure, you can generate thousands of unique variations. You just set up your basic rhythm and layer on some notes that trigger occasionally; as Stochastik runs, the Stochastik Process will run the combinatorics and produce dynamic beats that sound great and keep it interesting.

To get started with this new style of drum programming, Stochastik comes stocked with tons of patterns ready to learn from and play. Are you into Dance, Hip-Hop, Rock, Jazz, Funk, Glitch or Techno music? We have loads of pre-made kits that are easy to get started. With the ability to load in your custom samples through iTunes File Sharing, you can make Stochastik sound just the way you want.

Finally, a drum machine that makes it easy to have tons of variation.
No more boring loops.
