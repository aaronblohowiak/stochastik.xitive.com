What makes Stochastik different?

Stochastik is a new kind of drum machine where you set a probability for each step.    Now, you can express musical concepts like: "have the snare on the upbeat of 3 about 30% of the time."    You can generate millions of variations from a single pattern.

Finally, a drum machine that makes it easy to have tons of variation. No more boring loops.
